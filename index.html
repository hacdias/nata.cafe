☕️ Nata.café

Nata.café is a unix server maintained by @hacdias that provides some services for personal use and for friends. It is named after the portuguese custard tart, pastel de nata, which pairs very nicely with a nice cup of espresso. And it looks like this:

The services available at this server:

  • Miniflux
  • Nitter (soon)

It is very unfortunate that there is no emoji for pastéis de nata, or, more specifically, for the larger category of patries: the egg custard tarts. We proposed an Egg Tart emoji to Unicode in 2021, but it was rejected.
